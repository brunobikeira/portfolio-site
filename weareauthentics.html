We are Authentics

Ireland (UK) 2023

Branding, UX, UI Design, Front End Development

We are Authentics fosters personal growth and inclusivity for individuals with special needs through the transformative power of visual arts and writing.

As a designer, stepping into We are Authentics' realm unveils a unique set of objectives and challenges. My mission was to weave design that bridges gaps and empowers. My challenges include crafting an inclusive visual identity, translating empowerment into aesthetics, and fostering community connection through design.

Branding

We are Authentics' brand journey started with capturing the essence of empowerment and inclusivity. The brief called for a visual identity that would reflect their commitment to nurturing unique abilities and fostering personal growth.

The Logo

We are Authentics logo features a captivating silhouette of a walking child, exuding a sense of movement and progress. The silhouette is accompanied by a dynamic array of colorful circles, representing the diverse talents and potential that We are Authentics aims to unlock in individuals. Set against a sleek light background, the logo exudes a sense of sophistication and mystery, drawing attention to the vibrant and transformative elements within. The text is rendered in an inspiring shade of blue, emphasizing the organization's commitment to empowerment and growth.

Black & White Logo Versions

Even in its simplest form, our logo remains a powerful representation of Authentics' mission. In the black and white versions, the silhouette of a walking child and the dynamic circles retain their impactful presence. Stripped of color, the logo symbolizes the strength and inclusivity that lie at the core of our brand. The black and white iterations exude elegance and timelessness, ensuring our identity remains strong and recognizable in any setting. Whether in vibrant color or classic monochrome, our logo embodies the unwavering commitment to empowering hearts and nurturing minds, one step at a time.

Alternative Logo Versions

The alternate vertical lock-up is meant to be used in circumstances where larger emphasis should be made on the logomark.

A version of the logo using only the logotype is also permitted using the specific brand colors.

Color Palette

We are Authentics color palette embodies the brand's essence and vision. It signifies strength, inclusivity, passion, compassion, creativity, growth, and joy. Each color holds a purpose in conveying We are Authentics values, fostering empowerment, and nurturing personal development. Together, they create a harmonious palette that reflects the transformative and inclusive nature of We are Authentics.

Primary and Secondary Fonts

We are Authentics' choice of fonts effortlessly balances professionalism with creative expression. The primary font (Mansalva) adds a touch of artistic personality and playfullness to the brand's visual language. While The secondary font (Rubik) conveys reliability.
